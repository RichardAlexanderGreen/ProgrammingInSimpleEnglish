Turtles

Example Dialog / Script

---
Role: Draftsman.
Action: Make flower from a spiral.
. Make 100 turtles facing different directions.
. Set distance D to 128 mm.
. Set angle A to 10 degrees.
. All turtles put pen down.
. Repeat 6 times:
. . All turtles move distance D.
. . All turtles turn angle A to the right.
. . Divide distance D in half.

Action: Fractile tree.
. Set P to point at (0,0).
. Set L to 128 mm.
. Set A to 30 degrees.
. Draw fractile branch of length L and angle A at point P.

To: Draw fractile branch of length L and angle A at point P.
. If L is less than 3 mm:
. . Do nothing. (Quit the recursion.)
. Otherwise:
. . Make turtle at P with pen down.
. . Turn turtle (A/2) degrees left.
. . Move turtle distance L.
. . Draw fractile branch of length (L/2) and angle A at point (turtle location).
. . Move turtle to P with pen up.
. . Turn turtle A degrees right.
. . Move turtle distance L.
. . Draw fractile branch of length (L/2) and angle A at point (turtle location).
---
